About MNNIT Dance culture

 
 MNNIT has a vast trend of dance .There are communities of dance showing different dance forms of India.
                    these communities gave the idea of unity that even we have such variations in our dance forms but we stand together 
                     when it comes to nation.
                    these communities represent the dance forms of different states.
                    * Rajasthani Community represents the dance form ghoomar due to which Rajasthan has a different place in India.
                    * Punjabi Community represents the world famous dance form Bhangra which is the love of every indian.
                    * Marathi community represents the famous maharathi dance form lavani.
                    * gujrati cummunity represents the world famous garba and dandi.
              Along with this there are various dance groups also which represents both indian and western dance culture like 
                * Kathak group.
                * western dance group.
